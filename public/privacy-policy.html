Privacy Policy

How SiddhaConnect protects and uses your data

Overview

SiddhaConnect is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it secure.

Information We Collect

  • Device Information: We may collect device model, OS version, and Android ID for user identification and analytics.
  • Camera Access: Used only to capture and upload documents or reports within the app.
  • Location (if applicable): Used to verify presence during submissions, not stored or shared externally.

How We Use Your Data

All collected data is used strictly for internal operations such as tracking performance, submitting reports, and improving user experience. We do not sell or share user data with third parties.

Data Retention & Security

Your data is retained only as long as necessary for functionality and compliance. We implement strong security practices to safeguard your information.

User Control

You can revoke camera or location permissions at any time through your device settings. However, some features may stop working as expected.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the app after changes implies agreement with the updated terms.

Contact

For questions or concerns, please contact us at support@siddhaconnect.in.
